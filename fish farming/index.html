About Us

Regmi Fish Farm, located in the serene Patharvitta, Birtamode, Nepal, is a beacon of sustainable aquaculture in the region. Established with a vision to provide fresh, high-quality fish while preserving the environment, our farm blends traditional Nepali farming wisdom with modern techniques. We are dedicated to delivering fish that are not only delicious but also responsibly sourced, ensuring a minimal ecological footprint.

At the heart of our operations is our commitment to sustainability. We employ eco-friendly practices such as natural water filtration and organic feed to maintain the health of our fish and the surrounding ecosystem. Our farm supports the local community by providing employment opportunities and fostering education about sustainable aquaculture, making us a trusted name in Nepal’s fish farming industry.

Meet Our Founder: Regent Regmi

Regent Regmi, the visionary behind Regmi Fish Farm, brings decades of expertise in aquaculture and a deep passion for sustainable farming. Regent founded the farm to promote eco-conscious practices and provide fresh, healthy fish to Nepali households. His leadership ensures that every aspect of our operations aligns with our core values of quality, sustainability, and community support.

Our diverse range of fish is a testament to our commitment to quality and variety. We proudly farm nine species, each selected for their unique flavors and culinary versatility. From the tender, flavorful Rohu to the rich, seasonal Naini, our fish cater to a wide range of tastes and traditional Nepali recipes. Other species include Catla, known for its bold flavor; Grass Carp, a sustainable choice with a mild taste; Tilapia, versatile for various dishes; Pangasius, with its flaky texture; Common Carp, adaptable to many cooking methods; Silver Carp, ideal for steaming; and Bighead Carp, perfect for hearty soups and grilling.

At Regmi Fish Farm, we believe in building a sustainable future. By prioritizing eco-friendly practices and community engagement, we aim to set a standard for aquaculture in Nepal. Whether you’re a home cook or a restaurant owner, we invite you to explore our offerings and join us in celebrating the rich flavors of sustainably farmed fish.
